About

Joshua Spigelman is a computer science engineer who goes to the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor.

He was most recently an intern at Delphi Automotive’s Lab in Mountain View, CA where he worked with the autonomous vehicle team. He has also worked with the Electronics and Safety division of Delphi in Auburn Hills, MI.

His other work experience includes a summer as a camp counselor at the Jewish Community Center of Metro Detroit, and volunteer work with his high school’s robotics team.

In his free time, Joshua performs stand-up comedy with his college group.
